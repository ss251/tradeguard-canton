6 · EXCEPTION / DISPUTE the path most teams forget — shows operational realism
▢ TradeGuard
Action needed
Trade #TG-0039 — delivery not attested by deadline
Settlement did NOT occur. Cash and title both remain with original owners (atomicity = no half-settled state).
Resolution options
Extend deadline
Cancel & release
Escalate to regulator
Why it matters: TradFi judges ask "what happens when it goes wrong?" Having a dispute path = practitioner credibility. Most demos only show the happy path and lose points here.
